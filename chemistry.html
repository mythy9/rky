About the Chemistry Department

The Department of Chemistry at RKY College offers state-of-the-art laboratories, experienced faculty, and a curriculum designed to foster scientific thinking and discovery. Our students engage in groundbreaking research that contributes to advancements in various chemical disciplines.

Only the best for your Chemistry Enthusiasts!

Laboratory Facilities

Our chemistry department boasts state-of-the-art laboratories equipped with the latest tools for research and experimentation.

Analytical Lab

Equipped with spectrophotometers, chromatographs, and analytical balances.

Dedicated to organic synthesis with fume hoods and distillation setups.

Features calorimeters, viscometers, and other advanced instruments.

Research Lab

Open to postgraduate students for innovative projects and publications.
